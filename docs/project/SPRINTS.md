# Sprint Schedule

## Sprint Structure

- **Duration:** 2 weeks
- **Velocity target:** 30–40 story points
- **Ceremonies:**
  - Monday Week 1: Sprint kickoff (30 min)
  - Wednesday Week 1: Grooming (60 min)
  - Friday Week 2: Sprint review + retrospective (90 min)
